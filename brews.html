BREWSKIES

We source our beers from a monthly rotating list of breweries from the Treasure Valley. Take a look at who we've got lined up for the 2023 season (*FYI, this list can change). If you've got any suggestions on brews or breweries you think should be added to the list, head here and let us know!

*Note: the winter months (January, February (potentially November), and December), it's always hard to tell what condition the trails will be in. We don't want to encourage users to come drink suds with us and ruin our wonderful trails. However, if the trails are useable, we will be serving drinks! The early months we will have local coffee houses rotating thru instead of brews! Come support some wonderful roasteries and warm-up with a hot cup o' joe if the trails allow!
